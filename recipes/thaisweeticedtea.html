Thai Sweet Iced Tea

Description

A delicious Thai iced tea for a refreshing afternoon

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons Thai tea leaves
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅙ teaspoon crushed cardamom pods
  • ⅚ teaspoon crushed cloves
  • ⅚ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup and 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • ¼ cup and 2 teaspoons condensed milk
  • crushed ice, as needed

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in tea leaves, vanilla extract, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. Cover and remove from the heat. Let steep for 15 minutes.
  2. Pour tea through a fine mesh strainer; discard tea leaves. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  3. Fill glasses with crushed ice. Pour tea into glasses and top with condensed milk.
